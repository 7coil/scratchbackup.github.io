How to find your project


Welcome to the site! We archive random projects, and projects that are requested, so you can always have a backup stored somewhere safe. In order to find your project, you can go to https://scoldercreations.github.io/scratcharchive/projects/1 and change the "1" to the ID of your project.
Then, you should download the contents of the webpage as a .txt or .json, and upload them to Scratch! As long as you're connected to the internet, it'll be able to load the assets of your project.

Want your project backed up? We got you covered.


Scratch is developed by the Lifelong Kindergarten Group at the MIT Media Lab. See the scratch website.
